How can you help?

Consult Individuals

Talking to people often in trouble or stress in person and a private space often helps them. Contact us if you are willing to do us and we will contact you whenever we need your help

Conduct Motivational Sessions

Certain people do not like to share their problems with anyone, not even in private and at times can't handle the mental pressure. Such people, however, can be influenced through small gatherings. If you are a motivational speaker and would like to help, contact us

Conduct Rehab Activities

Activities like meditation, sports, art, music, etc. often help people forget their problems and focusing their minds on a particular object, thought or activity to achieve a mentaly clear and emotionally calm state. If you are someone who conducts such activites, contact us

Want to help us but don't have enough time? Help us financially

We always appreciate people who reach to us and express their will to help us by conducting seminars, consultation sessions, etc. But not everyone can do these, and yet wish to help. We regularly conduct speaker sessions, personal chat sessions, activity sessions, etc. for which we need financial aid. You can always help us indirectly by donating us
